A few words about us

As we age, our needs change, and it's important to have the necessary care and support in place to maintain a good quality of life. NIVASHINI ASSISTED CARE SERVICES encompasses a wide range of services and support systems to help older adults maintain their independence and dignity.This includes everything from in-home care and assistance with activities of daily living to specialized care.

In-home care includes personal care, such as bathing, grooming, and dressing, as well as household tasks like cooking, cleaning, and running errands.

Assisted living is a boon for older adults who need some level of assistance with activities of daily living, but who still want to maintain their independence.

Our aim is to provide overall support for Elders and Patients in need of assistance for day to day activities. Our dedicated team gives your parents a personalized care filling your shoes when you can't be with them due to unavoidable situations. International patients are taken care by our personalized Care coordinator in fulfilling their overall requirements during the treatment period. You can rest happily knowing that our team is supporting your loved one's in overall home healthcare services. We can help you with diagnostics at home, doctor home visits, trained attendants at home, physiotherapy at home, pharmacy, and much more. Our Care Coordinator assist your loved one's by visiting them frequently.

No matter what type of elder care services you or your loved ones may need, it's important to choose a provider that has the expertise, experience, and compassion to provide the best possible care. With the right support and care from NIVASHINI ASSISTED CARE SERVICES your Parents can continue to lead fulfilling and meaningful lives well into their golden years.
