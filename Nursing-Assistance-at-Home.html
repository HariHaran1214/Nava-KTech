Home attendant:

Home attendant will be assisting your parents with activities of daily living (ADLs): This includes helping patients with tasks such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting.

Taking vital signs: Attendant may take patients' vital signs, such as blood pressure, temperature, and pulse rate.

Moving and positioning patients: They will assist patients in moving, turning, and positioning in bed or in a chair to prevent bedsores and promote circulation.

Feeding patients : Home attendant help patients eat and drink, and ensure that they receive adequate nutrition.

Documenting patient information: They may record patient information such as vital signs, food and fluid intake, and bowel movements. In case of prior instructions given.

Assisting with patient transfers: Home attendants may help move patients from beds to wheelchairs or stretchers for transport to other areas of the hospital or to other facilities.

Home attendant play a vital role in helping patients maintain their health and independence

We provide you Home attendants from our third party vendors who are all background verified. Their identity verification documents will be provided on demand.

Cleaning service/Maid service

Our Maid service is a professional cleaning service that provides cleaning and maintenance services for residential and commercial properties. Maid services can range from basic cleaning tasks such as dusting, vacuuming, and mopping, to more extensive services such as deep cleaning, organizing, and laundry. Maid services can be scheduled on a regular basis, such as weekly or bi-weekly, or can be arranged for one-time cleanings.

Home Cook:

Need a personal home cook to prepare meals for you?

We got your back...

All your taste buds & health considered delicious cooking is now easily accessible by appointing our home cooks at your home.

Here are some common responsibilities our home cook do for you & your family

Meal planning: Home cook plan out the meals according to your household needs. This includes deciding on the dishes, ingredients, and portion sizes. You can provide your suggestions on your requirements

Food preparation: Our cook is responsible for preparing and cooking the meals. This includes chopping, sauteing, grilling, baking, and any other cooking methods required.

Food storage and organization: After preparing the meals, they will store the food properly to ensure they stay fresh. They may also organize your kitchen and pantry to make it easier to find ingredients and cooking tools.

Dietary considerations: If anyone in your household has dietary restrictions or preferences, you will need to inform our cook person in advance to take these into account when planning and preparing meals.

Your Responsibility: You will need to budget your food expenses and make sure of spending on groceries.

Overall, our home cook take responsibilities to ensure that your household is well-fed and happy.
