Home Blood Collection

  • Home
  • Home Blood Collection

Home blood test collection is a convenient and non-invasive way to have blood tests done without having to visit a medical laboratory or clinic.

Home blood test collection is becoming increasingly popular as people seek more control over their healthcare and want to avoid the inconvenience and expense of visiting a doctor's office or lab. It is also a useful option for people with mobility issues or those who live in remote areas.

At Nivashini Assisted care services,We have tie ups with third party vendors who are running registered licenced laboratories. Our trained staffs visit your home and collect small sample blood from a person's fingertip or a vein using a kit provided by the testing company, and then send the sample to the laboratory for analysis.

We provide accurate & quick results. We have qualified technicians to ensure that the test is appropriate and that the results are properly interpreted. We offer you Discounts on certain blood test. Home ECG test also available for your convenience. We have tie ups with the best laboratories in our locality get affordable tests at the convenience of your home & move towards healthy living
